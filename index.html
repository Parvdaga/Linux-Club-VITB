Events Archive.

Third Eye Quest

Calling all cyber sleuths! Third Eye Quest, part of AdVITya fest, challenged you to a thrilling two-phase adventure (February 19th & 22nd, 2024). You cracked cryptic codes and navigated online mazes in "Digital Infiltration" (Phase 1). You then pushed your limits in the offline "Extraction Point" (Phase 2) for top-secret intel. You competed, collaborated, and conquered this adrenaline-pumping quest!

Silent Disco

AdVITya Fest's Silent Disco on February 23rd, 2024, was a vibrant event filled with music and dance. Participants grooved to various genres using headphones, creating a unique, silent yet energetic atmosphere. Face-painting and a photo booth added to the merriment, making it a truly memorable experience for all involved. From enthusiastic dancers to dedicated volunteers, the event's spirit remained high until the very end.
